Windows：echo %PROCESSOR_ARCHITECTURE%

AMD64 → 选 amd64
ARM64 → 选 arm64

macOS：arch
arm64       → 选 Apple Silicon
i386/x86_64 → 选 Intel

Linux：uname -m
x86_64  → 选amd64
aarch64 → 选arm64


amd64   x86_64/x64      Intel Core、AMD Ryzen 等传统 PC/服务器 CPU
arm64   AArch64         Apple Silicon（M1/M2/M3）、高通 Snapdragon X Elite、AWS Graviton、树莓派 4/5（64位系统）等
